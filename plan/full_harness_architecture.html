Implementation-dependent gaps: (1) normalisation baselines — resolved P3: clamp-based with four DimensionTypes (ratio/composite/entropy/match_confidence); (2) CRITICAL_THRESHOLD — resolved P3: 0.2; (3) CAUTION_THRESHOLD — resolved P3: 0.4; (4) recovery strategy selection heuristics and softmax weights; (5) evidence sufficiency thresholds by claim scope; (6) tool reliability envelope calibration; (7) dep_class_gap_estimate calibration — relegated to advisory until empirical validation; (8) cannot_make_progress() threshold constants — resolved P6: STALL_WINDOW=5, MAX_SWITCHES=3, RECURRENCE_THRESHOLD=3, OSCILLATION_WINDOW=6; (9) PESSIMISTIC_THRESHOLD — resolved P4: 0.5; (10) journal_retention N size; (11) elimination_policy K retention size and posterior FLOOR; (12) VOI threshold — expected uncertainty reduction × decision impact; (13) adversarial prior seed quality — causal proximity filter is directional but imperfect.
The architectural principle Diagnostic health vectors (belief · coverage · verification · execution) replace the multi-axis signal bus. resolve_control_state() outputs NORMAL/CAUTIOUS/BLOCKED — a small, auditable surface. The five-tier implementation detail lives inside that function, not at the top level. generation_id double-increment per iteration preserves the two-substep model without named sub-step counters. Experience store reuses structures (decompositions, workflows, plans, sequences) not just probability priors. VOI gates additional evidence gathering. All v15 invariants — normalisation contract, deadlock detection, elimination policy, parallel concurrency model, adversarial reviewer, pre-execution review gate — remain present as implementation detail inside their respective nodes. The hard problems — calibrating thresholds, choosing VOI weights, populating the failure mode library, seeding the adversarial prior — remain implementation-dependent and are honestly labelled.
